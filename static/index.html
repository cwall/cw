About Waldron Inc.

Learn about the man named Carl.

I live in a constant state of "Let me create something dammit!". It would be horrible if it wasn't so motivating. I have a fervent need to build, design, draw, make, create. It's THE driving force in my small corner of life.

So how and what do I create you ask? First off, that's a great question, thanks for asking.

I scratch that creative bug by lending my talents to people who need things designed, developed, drawn or made. I build websites using my many years of web design and front-end development experience (around 10+ years). I design and deliver brands for companies large and small. I draw up user experience wireframes, create icons, build CMS systems using Django (and soon Ruby on Rails). I have been involved with every step of a creative project from brainstorm to final deliverable which has allowed me to cultivate the skills necessary to be an asset to any part of the process.

The short answer is I love to make things (and often stuff), I love to help people (and dogs), and I love to do push-ups. It's in my DNA.

I am a creative mutant. Nice to meet you.
